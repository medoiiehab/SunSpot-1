Power Your Day With Solar

SUNSPOT is the future of charging. Combining innovative sunflower solar technology, secure charging lockers, and seamless connectivity—all powered by the sun.

Why SUNSPOT?

Sunflower Solar Design

Our revolutionary sunflower-inspired solar panel automatically follows the sun throughout the day, increasing energy efficiency by up to 40% compared to traditional static solar panels. This adaptive technology ensures maximum power generation in any weather condition.

Secure Charging Locker

Every device is protected in our PIN-based secure locker system. Charge your smartphones and tablets safely while you're on the go. Weatherproof storage ensures your devices are safe while being powered by clean, renewable solar energy.

Connected WiFi & Hotspot

Stay always connected with dual connectivity options. Purchase premium WiFi directly through SUNSPOT or connect to your personal hotspot using your existing ISP package. Flexible, reliable, and always available connectivity.
